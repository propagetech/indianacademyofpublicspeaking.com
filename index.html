About us

Symphysis Training Pvt Ltd is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company. It owns the brand
Indian Academy of Public Speaking .
The organisation's core business is to provide training on public speaking or oratory skills to Adults & Children. We have tailor made courses for Professionals, Corporates and Children in age group of 8 to 12 years, 13 to 18 years and above respectively.
We also undertake coaching for corporates or individuals on Public Speaking, Executive Presence, Leadership skills and various topics on soft skills.

Fear of speaking in public also known as Glossophobia is considered to be a significant fear amongst many.
Taking this cue, we decided to focus our energies & abilities to help individuals eradicate this fear with our flagship Public Speaking Program
